FONTLOG for the Nepomuk font family
=====================================

This file provides detailed information on the Nepomuk Font Software. This information should be distributed along with the Nepomuk fonts and any derivative works.

The Nepomuk Font Software is a family consisting of two fonts: Nepomuk Regular, and Nepomuk Italic.


Basic font information
----------------------
Nepomuk is a serif text font family designed for use in music notation scores, reminiscent of the classic Modern and Century font families.
